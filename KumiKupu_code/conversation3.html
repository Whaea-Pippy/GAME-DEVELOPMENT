He Wheako Ataahua — Te Kōrero mō ngā Orca

HemiKia ora e Miriama! I pēhea tō ata?
Hi Miriama! How was your morning?
MiriamaTēnā koe e Hemi! I tino rawe tōku ata!
Hello Hemi! My morning was amazing!
HemiĀe rā? He aha i rawe ai?
Really? Why was it amazing?
MiriamaI te hīkoi ahau i te tahataha o te pūwaha, ā, i kite au i ngā orca e kau ana!
I was walking along the cliff edge beside the estuary, and I saw orcas swimming!
HemiE hia ngā orca i kite koe?
How many orcas did you see?
MiriamaE whā! E whā ngā orca e kau ana i te wai, tino tata ki te takutai.
Four! There were four orcas swimming close to the shore.
HemiKa mau te wehi! He tata rānei ki a koe?
Wow! Were they close to you?
MiriamaĀe, i tino tata! I te kau hoki ētahi kaihoe papa reti wai i tō rātou taha!
Yes, really close! Some paddle boarders were right beside them!
HemiI rongo au mō tērā i runga i Pukamata! Nā wai i tuku?
I heard about that on Facebook! Who posted it?
MiriamaNāku i tuku, ā, he maha hoki ngā tāngata i kōrero mā te waea me te kanohi ki te kanohi.
I did, and lots of people were talking about it on the phone and in person too.
HemiI pērā rawa te ātaahua o te rā?
Was it really that beautiful a morning?
MiriamaĀe, he rā mārie, he rā kanapa, he wai māhorahora. He ataahua rawa atu!
Yes, it was calm, sunny, and the water was smooth. So beautiful!
HemiMe kī, he wheako whakamīharo tēnā!
What an incredible experience!
MiriamaĀe, ka kore au e wareware i tērā rā. He tino taonga tō tātou moana!
Yes, I’ll never forget that day. Our ocean is such a treasure!
