Hararei — Mixed Tense Sentence Order

I te wiki tuatahi o ngā hararei, i haere ahau ki te pāka.

In the first week of the holidays, I went to the park.

I te wiki tuarua o ngā hararei, i noho ahau i te kāinga.

In the second week of the holidays, I stayed at home.

I te tīmatanga o ngā hararei, i toro ahau i te whare o taku kuia.

At the start of the holidays, I visited my grandmother’s house.

Ā te tīmatanga o ngā hararei, ka toro ahau i te whare o taku kuia.

At the start of the holidays, I will visit my grandmother’s house.

Ā te wiki tuarua o ngā hararei, ka haere ahau ki te pāka.

In the second week of the holidays, I will go to the park.

Ā te wiki tuatoru o ngā hararei, ka whakanui ahau i te Kirihimete.

In the third week of the holidays, I will celebrate Christmas.
