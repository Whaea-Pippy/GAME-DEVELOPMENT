Te Reo Flashcards — Extended Conversation

ArohaKia ora e Hemi! Kei te hiamo koe mō ngā hararei Kirihimete?
Hi Hemi! Are you excited about the Christmas holidays?
HemiĀe, tino nui! Ā koe, e Aroha?
Yes, heaps! And you, Aroha?
ArohaĀe, kāore e taea te tatari! Ka haere au ki te takutai me tōku whānau.
Yes, I can’t wait! I’m going to the beach with my family.
HemiKa rawe! Ka noho au ki te kāinga.
Cool! I’m going to stay home.
ArohaĀe? He aha ō mahere mō ngā rā hararei?
Yeah? What are your plans for the holidays?
HemiKa mātaki kiriata au, ka haere ki te papa tākaro, ā, ka kai aihikirīmi ia rā!
I’ll watch movies, go to the playground, and eat ice cream every day!
ArohaĀtaahua tērā! He pai ki ahau te aihikirīmi i te raumati.
That sounds lovely! I love ice cream in summer.
HemiĀe, he reka rawa atu! Ā, me koe, ka noho ki hea?
Yes, it’s so delicious! And where will you be staying?
ArohaKa noho mātou ki tētahi whare noho tata ki te moana.
We’ll stay in a holiday house near the sea.
HemiTino pai tērā. Ka tākaro koe i te moana?
That’s awesome. Will you play in the sea?
ArohaĀe, ka kaukau au, ka hī ika hoki, me te tākaro ki te onepū.
Yes, I’ll swim, go fishing, and play in the sand.
HemiKa pai! Kia pai tō wā ki te takutai.
Great! Have an awesome time at the beach.
ArohaĀe, ā, kia pai hoki tō wā whakatā. Kaua e kai nui rawa te aihikirīmi!
Thanks, and you too! Don’t eat too much ice cream!
HemiHaha, kāore e taea e au te oati i tērā! Hei konā rā, e hoa!
Haha, I can’t promise that! Bye, my friend!
ArohaHei konā!
Bye!
