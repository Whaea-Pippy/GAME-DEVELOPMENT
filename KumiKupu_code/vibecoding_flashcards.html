Vibecoding Flashcards

"Awhi"
To support or embrace someone in their coding journey.
"Kāpai"
When your code works perfectly, that's a "kāpai" moment.
"Mahi"
The work or effort you put into coding.
"Tautoko"
To support or endorse a fellow coder's idea.
"Whānau"
Your coding community or team.
"Wānanga"
A deep discussion or meeting about a coding project.
"Hā"
To give life to a project with your unique coding style.
"Mana"
The respect and authority you earn through your coding skills.
"Mauri"
The essential life force or vibe of a coding project.
"Kia kaha"
Stay strong and persevere when debugging.
